## Why BSL?

We wanted a license that:

1. Lets anyone use Vulis freely inside their organization — including on
   production lines — without paying.
2. Prevents a competitor from simply reselling Vulis or wrapping it as a
   hosted service.
3. Becomes genuinely open source after a reasonable delay.

No OSI-approved license can do all three (the Open Source Definition forbids
restricting resale). The BSL 1.1 is the industry-standard source-available
license that achieves this balance; it is used by Sentry, CockroachDB,
HashiCorp, MariaDB, and others.

See [ADR 0001](../ADR/0001-license.md) for the full rationale.
